Featured Brewery

Bear Republic Brewing Company

Located in the heart of wine country of California, Bear Republic has been serving quality beer since 1995. The brewery not only provides some of the highest quality beer in the state of California, but also serves awarding winning food. Located on the corner of Healdsburg’s historic downtown square, this local brewery is packing a punch of flavor and presenting it with a smile.

Bear Republic's beer is brewed using time tested and proven methods that are known to make their company special. By only using hand selected ingredients, this insure the quality of taste, smell, feel and look. If you are interested in good quality beer, we suggest you check these guys out!
